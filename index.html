Tico Logistics

We are dedicated to delivering the most effective logistics solutions to and from Vietnam. TICO’s philosophy centers on building trust with customers, recognizing it as the cornerstone of our success. With a focus on professionalism, modernization, speed, commitment, and support, we are commited to ensuring our clients’ satisfaction and trust.

Services

Sea Freight

As a a leading freight forwarder in Vietnam that has been granted NVOCC status (FMC No. 028018), TICO specializes in providing comprehensive sea freight services to and from all major regions of the world. Our experienced team ensures that your cargo is transported safely, efficiently, and on time, while keeping costs under control.

Air Freight

TICO is a reliable air freight forwarder in Vietnam, providing efficient and cost-effective air cargo services to and from all major regions in the world. With a team of experienced professionals and a vast network of partners and carriers, we can handle all types of air shipments, including urgent, oversized, and dangerous goods.

Rail Freight
