Air Freight

TICO is a leading freight forwarder in Vietnam that has been granted Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier status (FMC No. 028018). As an NVOCC, we have the authority to issue our bills of lading, which allows us to provide a higher level of control and flexibility over our sea freight services.

We specialize in providing comprehensive sea freight solutions for businesses of all sizes, with a particular focus on delivering cost-effective and reliable services to and from all major regions of the world. Our team of experienced professionals understands the complexities of sea freight and can help you navigate the challenges of international shipping.

Whether you need to ship full container loads, less-than-container loads, or oversized cargo, TICO can provide the expertise and resources you need to get your goods to their destination on time and within budget. We offer a full range of services, including documentation, customs clearance, cargo insurance, and more, to ensure that your shipment arrives at its destination safely and efficiently.

At TICO, we pride ourselves on our commitment to communication and transparency. We keep you informed every step of the way, providing regular updates and tracking information to ensure that you always know where your shipment is and when it will arrive.

With our NVOCC status, extensive network of partners and carriers, and dedicated team of professionals, TICO is your ideal partner for all your sea freight needs.

Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you streamline your logistics operations.
